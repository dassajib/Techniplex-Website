Our Mission


Techniplex is one of the leading and fastest-growing business conglomerates in Bangladesh. It is a world-class healthcare instruments provider and distribution house in Bangladesh working since 2015. From its inception, Techniplex has been well- structured and fine-tuned in promoting and establishing leading brands of internationally reputed companies.

With excellent relationship with the service providers, entrepreneurs and the users of the medical industry Techniplex is playing a vital role in the marketing & distribution of medical devices and instruments to meet the local market demand.
